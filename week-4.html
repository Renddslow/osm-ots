The Nevi'im in the Canon

The Nevi'im have been intentionally positioned not just to tell us the continued story of the Israelites, but to explain to us why...

  • Israel has a monarchy
  • The Northern Kingdom is lost
  • The Exile occurred

The Nevi'im in the Canon

  • The Nevi'im offers a minority view of the trajectory of Israel
  • The Nevi'im does not look fondly on Israel's "greatest" kings
  • Not all of Israel's prophets represent the small contingent that make up the main characters of the Former Prophets nor the writers/teachers of the Latter Prophets
